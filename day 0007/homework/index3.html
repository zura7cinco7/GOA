about football

what is football

Football, also known as soccer in some countries, is a team sport played between two teams of eleven players on a rectangular field with a goal at each end. The objective is to score by getting the ball into the opposing teams goal. The team with the most goals at the end of the game wins

rules

The game is typically played over two 45-minute halves with a 15-minute halftime break.

The aim is to score more goals than the opponent by getting the ball into the opposing teams goal.

Each team has 11 players: 10 outfield players and 1 goalkeeper.

the best part of the football

teamwork and Tactical Play

Football is a team sport, and seeing a well-executed play or passing sequence can be incredibly satisfying. Great teamwork shows the strategy behind the game, and its a beautiful sight when a plan unfolds perfectly.

The Fans and Atmosphere

The passion and energy of the crowd make football games special. From chants to coordinated celebrations, fans bring life to the game, creating an atmosphere that can even boost players performances.
